About me

Hello, I am Denis, 14 years old, live in Germany and go to 8th grade. Besides my enthusiasm for programming, I have other interesting skills and characteristics. These language skills not only open up new opportunities for me in the field of programming, but also in the field of international cooperation and cultural exchange. One of my strengths is the ability to recognize and solve problems. This applies not only to programming, but also to everyday life. I am a team player and find it fulfilling to find creative solutions in group projects and to achieve goals together. I am grateful for the many opportunities that programming offers me and I am determined to pursue my passion. I am curious about future projects and challenges and look forward to continuing to do my best to develop my skills as a programmer.
